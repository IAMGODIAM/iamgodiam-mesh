Pillar IV · E5 Enclave

Engagement

Civic education, public policy literacy, media, storytelling, and mobilization.

The engagement pillar is where the lineage meets the public record. Stories, films, policy briefs, and community media — not as messaging, but as evidence. Block to the Boardroom is the flagship program: a curriculum and convenings series designed to move young people from the block — the street, the neighborhood, the ZIP code — to the boardroom, the ballot box, and the policy table.

Block to the Boardroom is in early development. The engagement pillar also includes E5's film production arm, the BDI data storytelling series, and the Coalition media infrastructure — dispatches, print issues, and the annual member convening.
