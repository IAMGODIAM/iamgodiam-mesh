The McCartney Academy is named for Ralph McCartney — a Liberty City elder, Overtown root, and community anchor who served as a security guard at Charles R. Drew K-8 during the years Israel Lee Armstead walked those halls. His oral history is on record at the University of Florida Samuel Proctor Oral History Program. The school named for him is not metaphor. It is debt repayment.

The McCartney Academy initiative has two tracks: Track A — academic programming anchored at FCI/MDC; Track B — a physical school building in Liberty City. The political window for Track B runs through February 14, 2027 (Superintendent Dotres lame-duck period). Outreach to 8 officials is complete. The McCartney Palace landmark nomination is in progress.
