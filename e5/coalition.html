§ A Coalition of the Willing

The
Coalition.

A network, not a hierarchy. Those who understand walk with us. Those who do not are not pursued.

What the Coalition is

The Coalition is not an audience. It is a covenant. A Coalition of the Willing is exactly what it names. Those who understand walk with us.

To be of the Coalition is to commit time, talent, and treasure. The Coalition grows the Coalition. The captains do not.

We do not negotiate with the question of whether the work is worth doing. We answer it by doing the work.

Two missions, one lineage

The Coalition includes Black-led organizations, individual builders, funders, partner institutions, and mission-aligned allies. Entry requires a covenant: a financial stake of any size, four convenings per year, eight hours of presence at the work, and an introduction of two others to what we are building.

The center holds, and the door is open to those who come through it on their own legs.

— The Covenant —

What it means to walk with us.

i.

A financial stake

A financial contribution of any size. The amount is not the commitment. The commitment is the commitment.

ii.

Four convenings

Four convenings per year, minimum. The Coalition assembles. Presence is not optional; it is the definition of membership.

iii.

Eight hours

Eight hours of presence at the work per year. Not observation. Presence. The work is done by those who show up for it.

iv.

Introduce two others

Introduce two others to what we are building. The Coalition grows the Coalition. The captains do not.
